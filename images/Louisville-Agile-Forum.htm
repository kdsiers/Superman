The Louisville Agile Forum consists of Agile practitioners in the greater Louisville area.  We like to share our Agile experiences, network and help others with challenges related to Agile adoption.

We talk about Scrum, eXtreme Programming, Lean software engineering and other topics related to software development.

This is a public forum open to executives, managers, product owners, scrummasters,, developers, testers - basically anyone interested in sharing and learning more about Agile methods!
